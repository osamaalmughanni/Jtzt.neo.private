# Built for companies that want control

Jtzt is a multi-tenant working-hours platform for internal company operations. It is designed to feel calm, fast, and dependable from the first screen to the last report.

Every company runs on its own SQLite database. That gives teams clear ownership, clean separation, and simple backup paths without operational noise.

## Why the model is stronger

- Each company has an isolated data boundary.
- The system stays easy to audit and maintain.
- Queries stay direct and predictable.
- Deployment stays light.

## What teams do with it

- Employees sign in and track time.
- Company admins manage users, projects, and tasks.
- System admins create and maintain companies.

## Why the architecture matters

Jtzt is built so the product can grow without becoming obscure. Frontend pages stay focused. Backend routes stay thin. Business logic lives in services. Shared types remain explicit.

That is what makes it scalable for internal operations and still understandable for engineers, operators, and AI agents.

## What it feels like

Jtzt is not trying to be a noisy all-in-one suite. It is meant to feel precise. A company signs in, tracks time, manages structure, and moves on.

The product stays compact. The system stays legible. The data stays yours.
